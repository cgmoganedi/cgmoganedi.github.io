Nutrition Plan

Nutrition forms an integral part of any living organism. You need energy and power to do anything, no system can work without a form of energy and your body is perfect example of a functional system. Therefore you need to eat healthy and feed your body with its daily nutrients for normal function of its internal operation.

3. 30 Push Ups (1 min)

4. 50 Lateral Legs (1 min)

5. 30 Push Ups (1 min)

6. 50 Horizontal Leg Flips (1 min)
